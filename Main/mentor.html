  Student Mentor Programme

Main Content

What

The Mentoring program, inspired by Counselling services at IIT Kanpur, was successfully launched at IIIT-Delhi, in the Monsoon semester ’14.

Why

The Mentoring program is designed to help first year students deal with the stress of adapting to a university campus. Mentors are expected to play friend, philosopher and guide to first year students in a variety of ways. They need to help defuse personal and academic crisis, and be available to counsel and help their assigned mentees with any issue personal, academic or institutional.

Mentors

Handpicked third and fourth year student candidates are interviewed by Dr. Raj Ayyar and the Student Council. Mentors were selected on the basis of various criteria such as a proven track record in service-learning, and other mentoring roles. The mentors are trained in basic counselling strategies by the Institute Counsellor, and this training will be reinforced periodically in booster sessions.
