  Frequently Asked Questions

Main Content

1&2

What is the program?

The change from school to college is drastic. The Student-Mentor program is aimed at helping students to resolve the problems which they might face in coping up with these changes.

Is there a fee to participate in the mentor program?

There is no fee involved in the program.

3&4

Who is a mentor?

Mentors are handpicked 3rd and 4th year students.

What kind of problems can I approach them for?

A mentee can approach their mentor regarding any problem which affects their life, no matter how trivial it seems.

5&6

Would my mentor discuss my problems with other people?

No, the mentor has to maintain complete confidentiality. No one else besides him would find out about your problems, without your consent.

Would this go on my record?

No. This will not appear on your record.

7&8

Will I be called crazy if I approach a mentor?

Not at all. Mentors are your seniors who help you with your college life.

What if I am not satisfied with the counselling given by my mentor?

Mentees are free to approach the meta-mentors or the counsellor at any time regarding any issue. This would be strictly confidential as well.
