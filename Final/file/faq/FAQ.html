Frequently Asked Questions

Main Content

1&2

What is the program?

The Student-Mentor program is aimed at helping students to resolve the problems which they face in their day-to-day lives....

Is there a fee to participate in the mentor program?

There is no fee involved in the program

3&4

Who is a mentor?

Mentors are handpicked 3rd and 4th year students.

What kind of problems can I approach them for?

A mentee can approach their mentor regarding any problem which affects their life, no matter how trivial it seems.

5&6

Would my mentor discuss my problems with other people?

No, the mentor has to maintain complete confidentiality. No one else besides him would find out about your problems, without your consent.

Would this go on my record?

No. This will not appear on your record.
